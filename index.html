FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What kind of medical services does Saravana Hospital provide? +

We're a multi-specialty hospital in Chennai offering surgical care, maternity and fertility services, general medicine, pediatrics, dermatology, and ENT treatment. We also have in-house lab, scanning, pharmacy, and 24/7 emergency services.

Do you have emergency services at night and on weekends? +

Yes, our emergency department never closes. Critical cases are seen immediately, and our goal is to have a doctor assess you within 15-20 minutes of arrival.

How do I book an appointment with a doctor? +

Call our reception desk, use our online booking form, or walk in during consultation hours. For urgent cases, we'll fit you in the same day.

Are vaccination services available for adults and children? +

Yes, our vaccine center provides complete immunization for all ages—from newborn vaccinations to adult boosters and travel vaccines. Both pediatric and adult vaccination schedules are available.

Do you have lab and scan facilities or do I need to go somewhere else? +

Everything's here—diagnostic lab, X-ray, ultrasound, ECG, pharmacy, and operation theatres. No need to travel elsewhere for tests or treatment.
